About Me

I’m a web designer/front end developer living in Santa Ana, California. I am currently working for Dhali, a Web Design company in Rancho Santa Margarita. I started working for Dhali on May 2005 before that I was attending The Art Institute of California – Orange County from October 2002 through December 2004 where I received an Associates of Science Degree in Interactive Media Design.

Building websites is what I love doing and I don't see myself doing anything else. Web Development is constantly evolving which in turn challenges you to improve your skills and learn new ones as well, that is one of the reasons why its such a great industry as you're always learning new tools and programming languages.
